# SEO Audit

Date: 2026-03-24  
Scope: repo inspection plus live production checks against `https://publicdatawatch.com`

## Executive Summary

PublicDataWatch is crawlable, but it is not yet presenting strong SEO signals to non-JavaScript crawlers.

The biggest issue is not content quality. It is metadata delivery.

Live raw HTML for `/`, `/about-us`, `/help`, `/combined-map`, and `/everett` currently ships with:

- the same generic `<title inertia>PublicDataWatch</title>`
- no server-visible meta description
- no canonical tag
- no Open Graph or Twitter card tags

That means the Vue `Head` tags help users after hydration, but they are not giving search crawlers a strong first-response metadata layer.

## What Is Working

- Public pages are reachable and indexable through normal `200` responses.
- `robots.txt` exists and is permissive.
- City landing routes now exist live, including `/everett`, `/boston`, `/chicago`, `/san-francisco`, `/new-york`, `/montgomery-county-md`, and `/seattle`.
- There is already meaningful public route surface for homepage, help, about, legal pages, news, maps, and city landings.

## Key Findings

### 1. No crawler-visible metadata layer

Evidence:

- [resources/views/app.blade.php](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/views/app.blade.php) only renders a generic `<title inertia>{{ config('app.name', 'Laravel') }}</title>`.
- Live HTML for `/` and `/everett` returns the generic title only, with no description or canonical tags.
- Vue pages do define titles in many places, but those tags are not visible in the first HTML response.

Impact:

- weak search snippets
- poor page differentiation for crawlers
- city pages cannot become strong landing pages with a generic shell title

### 2. No sitemap

Evidence:

- `https://publicdatawatch.com/sitemap.xml` returns `404`
- [public/robots.txt](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/public/robots.txt) does not reference a sitemap

Impact:

- slower discovery of public pages
- weaker crawl guidance for city pages, help content, and news pages

### 3. Homepage city cards are not feeding the city landing strategy

Evidence:

- [app/Http/Controllers/HomeController.php](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/app/Http/Controllers/HomeController.php) currently assigns each city card a `mapUrl` that points to `data-map` or `combined-map`
- local and live city landing routes exist through [routes/web.php](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/routes/web.php) and [app/Http/Controllers/CityLandingController.php](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/app/Http/Controllers/CityLandingController.php)

Impact:

- the homepage is not reinforcing the new city landing pages as primary indexable entry points
- internal linking is favoring tool pages over search-facing landing pages

### 4. Public page titles are often generic even at the component level

Evidence from page components:

- [resources/js/Pages/CityMapLite.vue](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/js/Pages/CityMapLite.vue) title is only the city name
- [resources/js/Pages/CombinedDataMap.vue](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/js/Pages/CombinedDataMap.vue) title is `Combined Data Map`
- [resources/js/Pages/DataMap.vue](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/js/Pages/DataMap.vue) title is `Single Data Map`
- [resources/js/Pages/RadialMap.vue](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/js/Pages/RadialMap.vue) title is `Home`
- [resources/js/Pages/GenericMap.vue](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/js/Pages/GenericMap.vue) title is `Home`
- [resources/js/Pages/News/Show.vue](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/js/Pages/News/Show.vue) sets a title, but no description

Impact:

- weak query matching
- poor snippet quality
- missed long-tail opportunity for city and data-specific searches

### 5. City landing pages are live, but still thin as SEO surfaces

Evidence:

- [app/Http/Controllers/CityLandingController.php](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/app/Http/Controllers/CityLandingController.php) supplies a short tagline and intro only
- [resources/js/Pages/CityMapLite.vue](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/js/Pages/CityMapLite.vue) is experience-first, but light on crawlable explanatory copy

Impact:

- city pages currently function more like compact interactive tools than search-focused landing pages
- they need stronger titles, descriptions, and a little more explanatory content before they are likely to rank well

### 6. Brand cleanup is incomplete

Evidence:

- [resources/js/Pages/Support/HelpContact.vue](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/js/Pages/Support/HelpContact.vue) still uses `help@bostonscope.com`
- [resources/js/app.js](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/js/app.js) still sets `const appName = 'BostonScope'`
- [resources/js/Components/AiAssistant.vue](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/resources/js/Components/AiAssistant.vue) still introduces itself as the BostonScope AI Assistant

Impact:

- brand inconsistency in search-facing and user-facing surfaces
- avoidable trust loss

## Recommended Low-Risk Improvements

### Immediate implementation candidates

1. Build a lightweight metadata mapper in the Laravel app shell.
   - Generate server-visible title, description, canonical, and social tags from `$page['component']`, `$page['url']`, and selected props.
   - Do this first for:
     - homepage
     - about
     - help
     - help-contact
     - subscription
     - city landing pages
     - news index
     - news article pages

2. Add a simple sitemap endpoint.
   - Include:
     - `/`
     - `/about-us`
     - `/help`
     - `/help/users`
     - `/help/municipalities`
     - `/help/researchers`
     - `/help/investors`
     - `/help-contact`
     - `/privacy-policy`
     - `/terms-of-use`
     - city landing pages
     - news index and news articles
   - Then add `Sitemap: https://publicdatawatch.com/sitemap.xml` to [public/robots.txt](/home/briarmoss/Documents/boston-ai-project/public/robots.txt).

3. Point homepage city cards to city landing pages.
   - Let the city landing page become the main search-facing entry point.
   - Keep the full map as the secondary click deeper in the funnel.

### Next wave after that

4. Rewrite weak page titles and descriptions.
   - Example shape for city pages:
     - `Everett Crime Map and Public Safety Data | PublicDataWatch`
   - Example shape for map pages:
     - `Interactive Public Data Map | PublicDataWatch`

5. Expand city landing copy slightly.
   - one concise paragraph on what data is included
   - one concise paragraph on how to use the page
   - preserve the lightweight feel

6. Clean public brand leftovers.
