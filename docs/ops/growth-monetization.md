# Growth And Monetization

## Purpose

Run pricing, distribution, and social execution through a conservative recommendation-and-approval loop that minimizes founder thrash while keeping brand and financial control in founder hands.

Use [social-distribution.md](./social-distribution.md) for the channel-specific operating playbook and page strategy.

## Primary Outcomes

- clear monetization experiments
- coherent pricing and messaging
- sustainable content cadence
- trackable social and funnel performance
- minimal founder effort beyond approval and external execution

## Scope

This area includes:
- pricing and package recommendations
- Stripe configuration planning
- social account creation and management plan
- content calendar and posting recommendations
- conversion CTA strategy
- coordination with analytics and SEO

## First Objectives

1. Audit current pricing and Stripe setup.
2. Define the first conservative monetization hypotheses.
3. Define which social channels are worth creating.
4. Build a content and execution cadence that ties into real product goals.

## Operating Model

Agent:
- proposes offers
- proposes CTA changes
- drafts posts and calendars
- proposes pricing and Stripe changes
- creates experiment definitions

Founder:
- approves pricing and public messaging
- executes external account actions
- creates or manages external social accounts
- applies Stripe dashboard changes when needed

## Experiment Rules

- one clear hypothesis per experiment
- one primary success metric
- low-risk implementation first
- rollback path defined before launch
- avoid overlapping changes that make attribution unclear

## Social Strategy Principles

- prioritize channels that can drive actual visits and conversions
- prefer consistency over volume
- tie posts to useful pages and offers
- do not confuse engagement with traction

## Inputs Required From Founder

- current Stripe setup and product list
- willingness to create and use specific social accounts
- constraints around brand voice and public positioning

## Agent-Driven Work

- pricing audit
- monetization roadmap
- social and content planning
- post drafting
- CTA and funnel recommendations

## Founder-Required Actions

- creating external accounts
- publishing posts
- approving and applying Stripe pricing/config changes
